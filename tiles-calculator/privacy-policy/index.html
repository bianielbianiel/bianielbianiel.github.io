Privacy Policy

Tiles Calculator

Last updated: March 2026

Tiles Calculator ("the App") is a free utility app developed by Daniel Bialek.

Data Collection and Local Storage

The App allows users to save job records containing customer details (name, phone number, email address, and address) that the user enters manually. All such data is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on the user's device.

This data is never transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party. No analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools are integrated into the App.

Contact Actions

The App provides buttons to call, text, or email a customer using the details the user has saved. These actions use standard Android intents to open the device's built-in phone dialler, SMS app, or email client.

The App does not read the device's contact list, call log, or message history, and does not request any permissions to do so.

Permissions

The App does not request any special device permissions (no camera, location, contacts, storage, internet, phone, or SMS permissions).

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App contains no ads, in-app purchases, or links to external websites.

Data Deletion

Users can delete individual job records within the App at any time. All data can also be removed by clearing the App's data in Android settings or by uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in an updated version of the App and on this page.
